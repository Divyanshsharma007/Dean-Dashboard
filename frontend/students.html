About EduPredict

EduPredict is an AI-powered Student Dropout Prediction & Management platform developed to help educational institutions proactively identify students who may be at risk of leaving their academic programs.

The system utilizes a CatBoost Machine Learning model trained on student academic indicators including attendance, cumulative GPA, academic performance trends, and other institutional data points to estimate dropout probability.

The objective is to enable Deans, Academic Advisors, Retention Teams, and University Administrators to take timely corrective action and improve student success outcomes through data-driven interventions.
