ABOUT THIS PLATFORM

THE TOOL

Student Dropout Prediction & Management

EduPredict is an AI-powered early warning system designed to identify students at risk of academic dropout before the situation becomes irrecoverable. The platform continuously analyses academic performance indicators — attendance patterns, CGPA trajectories, backlog accumulation, and fee compliance — to generate individual risk scores for every enrolled student.

The dashboard enables the Dean's Office and retention coordinators to prioritise outreach, allocate counselling resources efficiently, and track intervention outcomes across all academic departments. Rather than reactive remediation, the platform enables proactive, evidence-based student support.

THE MODEL

CatBoost Gradient Boosting Architecture

At the core of EduPredict is a CatBoost (Categorical Boosting) classification model — a gradient boosting algorithm developed by Yandex, well-suited to structured tabular data with categorical variables. CatBoost handles mixed data types natively, eliminates the need for manual one-hot encoding, and is particularly robust against overfitting on small-to-medium academic datasets.

The model was trained on multi-year institutional student records, incorporating features such as semester-wise GPA, cumulative attendance, fee payment history, backlog counts, and department-level academic load metrics. The result is a binary classifier that produces a calibrated dropout probability score for each student, updated at regular intervals throughout the academic year.
